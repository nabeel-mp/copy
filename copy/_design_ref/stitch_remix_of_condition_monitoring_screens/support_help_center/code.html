Frequently Asked Questions

local_shipping How do I track delivery delays?
expand_more

Logistics for construction materials can vary based on heavy vehicle traffic and site accessibility. You can track your real-time vehicle location in the 'My Orders' section. If a delay exceeds 2 hours, our automated compensation system triggers a priority handling flag.

  • Real-time GPS tracking enabled for all fleet.
  • SMS alerts sent for ETA shifts.
  • Direct driver contact available 30 mins before arrival.
verified What if the material quality is not up to spec?
expand_more
Doorcarts maintains a "Quality Guarantee" policy. If the delivered aggregate, cement, or steel does not match your ordered grade, do not sign the delivery note. Take a photo and upload it through the 'Raise Dispute' button in your order history for immediate inspection or replacement.
percent Can I get bulk discounts for large projects?
expand_more
Absolutely. For projects exceeding 50 metric tons or weekly material cycles, we offer Tiered Corporate Pricing. Please select 'Corporate Account' in your profile or email bulk@doorcarts.com with your BOQ (Bill of Quantities) for a custom quote within 4 hours.
